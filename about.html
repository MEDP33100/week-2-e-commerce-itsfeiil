Get to know about crystals

Amethyst Bracelet

Amethyst is a calming stone often associated with spiritual awareness, intuition, and peace. It can help reduce stress and enhance meditation.

Rose Quartz Bracelet

Rose Quartz is known as the stone of love, Rose Quartz promotes self-love, compassion, and emotional healing. It’s great for fostering deep emotional connections.

Clear Quartz

Clear Quartz is known as the "master healer" as it amplifies energy and brings clarity of thought. It is believed to promote healing in the body and mind.

Aventurine Bracelet

Aventurine is often associated with luck and prosperity. It’s also believed to bring emotional balance and promote harmony.

Citrine Bracelet

Citrine is a joyful, radiant stone believed to bring abundance, happiness, and personal power. It’s often used to manifest prosperity and success.

Sodalite Bracelet

Tiger's Eye Bracelet

Tiger's Eye symbolizes strength, courage, and confidence. It can help with decision-making, increasing personal power and willpower.

Labradorite Bracelet

Labradorite is a mystical stone, known for its beautiful play of color. It enhances mental clarity, intuition, and spiritual growth.

Black Tourmaline Bracelet

Black Tourmaline is a powerful protective stone shields against negative energy, providing grounding and emotional stability.
